Private Document Editor With No Upload

Edit DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, and CSV files locally in your browser without uploading documents to a server. Your files stay on your device from open to save.

Maximum Privacy — How It Works

ByBrowser runs the complete OnlyOffice office suite inside your browser using WebAssembly. When you open a document, the file is read directly by your browser's local file API — it is never transmitted over the network. The editing engine, format conversion, and file saving all happen in your browser tab with no backend involvement.

What Makes This Different From Google Docs or Office 365?

  • No upload — Google Docs and Office 365 send your file to cloud servers. ByBrowser does not.
  • No account — no sign-in, no email address, no usage tracking
  • No internet required after load — install as a PWA and edit documents fully offline
  • Open source — inspect the code, self-host, and verify the privacy claims yourself

Supported File Formats

  • DOCX — Microsoft Word documents
  • XLSX — Microsoft Excel spreadsheets
  • PPTX — Microsoft PowerPoint presentations
  • CSV — Comma-separated value files

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this editor designed for?
Anyone who handles sensitive documents — legal, medical, financial, or personal — and needs to edit them without sending the content to a third-party service.
Can I verify that no data is uploaded?
Yes. Open your browser's Network tab in DevTools while editing and you will see no document content being transmitted.
Is it truly free?
Yes. ByBrowser is free and open source under the AGPL-3.0 license. There are no paid tiers or usage limits.
How does it work technically?
The OnlyOffice document engine is compiled to WebAssembly and runs entirely in your browser tab. No server-side process is involved in opening, rendering, or saving your files.
