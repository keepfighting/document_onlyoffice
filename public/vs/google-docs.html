Frequently asked questions

Is this a Google Docs alternative without AI?

Yes. There are no AI features and nothing is trained on your documents. It is a plain, private document editor that runs locally in your browser.

Do I need a Google account or any account?

No account is required. There is no sign up and no login of any kind.

Are my documents stored in the cloud?

No. Everything runs client-side in your browser. Your files are never uploaded and stay on your device.

Is it open source?

Yes, it is open source under the AGPL-3.0 license and can be self-hosted from the source code on GitHub.
